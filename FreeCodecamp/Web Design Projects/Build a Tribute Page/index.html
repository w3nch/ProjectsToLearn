Dr. Norman Borlaug
Dr. Norman Borlaug in the field

Dr. Norman Borlaug was an American agronomist and humanitarian who played a crucial role in the Green Revolution, a series of agricultural innovations that saved over a billion people from starvation.

He is often referred to as the "Father of the Green Revolution" and was awarded the Nobel Peace Prize in 1970 in recognition of his contributions to humanity.
